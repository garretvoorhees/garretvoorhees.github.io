<div class="project-description">
<p>A living archive of typography and user interface design in video games.</p>
<p>Let’s celebrate the games that have thoughtfully considered the UI and typography as an important part of the game, and provide constructive criticism and analysis for those who haven’t. Let’s learn from the history of graphic design in games to make things better.</p>

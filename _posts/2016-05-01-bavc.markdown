<div class="project-description">
<p>The <em>Bay Area Video Coalition</em> (BAVC) is an awesome local non-profit that provides training for creative professionals, operates youth programs and generally supports creative people. I was responsible for the entire strategy and responsive design process, including new information architecture and a full visual redesign.</p>
<p>Work done while employed by <a target="_blank" href="https://www.chapterthree.com/">Chapter Three</a>.</p>
